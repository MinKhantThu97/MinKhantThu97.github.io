Our Drivers

Kris Meeke

Kris Meeke

Kris Meeke is a Northern Irish professional rally driver, best known for competing in the FIA World Rally Championship.

Jarmo Lehtinen

Jarmo Lehtinen

Jarmo Lehtinen is a rally co-driver from Finland. He was the co-driver to former World Rally Championship driver Mikko Hirvonen and have scored 15 WRC wins competing under Ford World Rally Team and Citroën Total.

Jarmo Lehtinen

Sébastien Loeb

Sébastien Loeb is a French professional rally, racing and rallycross driver. He is the most successful driver in the World Rally Championship, having won the world championship a record nine times in a row.

Jari-Matti Latvala

Jari-Matti Latvala

Jari-Matti Latvala is a Finnish rally driver who has competed in the World Rally Championship. His co-driver for most of his career was Miikka Anttila, who co-drove for Latvala between the 2003 Rallye Deutschland and 2019 Rally Catalunya.

Jari-Matti Latvala

Nicolas Gilsoul

Nicolas Gilsoul is a Belgian rally co-driver. He is the former co-driver for Thierry Neuville. After competing in regional events from 2000, Gilsoul made his international debut in 2003 when co-driving with Bruno Thiry.

Hayden Paddon

Hayden Paddon

Hayden Paddon is a New Zealand rally driver. He was PWRC world champion in 2011 and won the New Zealand Rally Championship in 2008, 2009, 2013, 2018 and 2021
